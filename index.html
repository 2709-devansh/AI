What is Artificial Intelligence??

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer sciences that emphasizes the development of intelligence machines, thinking and working like humans. For example, speech recognition, problem-solving, learning and planning.

What are it's applications?

Artificial Intelligence is used in several fields/ ADVANTAGES like:
1. In eCommerce, for Examples-Amazon, Flipkart, Ebay etc.
2. In Healthcare, like apps for buying medicines, and consulting a doctor through video chats etc.
3. In Cyber Security, like apps for monitoring CCTV cameras, detecting unauthorised or suspicous login activities in the confedential documents of a company etc.

What are it's disadvantages?

Artificial Intelligence also has several DISADVANTAGES like:
1. AI machines lack creativity
2. AI machines cannot improve with experience but humans cannot
3. AI machines have a hight cost of building, setup and implementation
4. AI Machines cannot develop a bond with humans which is an essential attribute when comes to Team Management
